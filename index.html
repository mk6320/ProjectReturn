The Addiction Trap

The dopamine loop keeps you stuck because it works... temporarily.

1. The Biological Trigger

It starts with a feeling you want to avoid—boredom, loneliness, or stress. Your brain craves a quick dopamine fix to numb the discomfort.

2. The Dissociation

You enter the fantasy. In this state, your brain releases real reward chemicals. You feel loved, powerful, and safe. Reality fades away.

3. The Reality Crash

Eventually, you must return. Reality now feels even duller by comparison. Guilt sets in, creating more stress, which triggers the loop all over again.

The Path Out

Our scientific method replaces the loop with real groundedness.

1. Interrupt the Autopilot

You cannot stop a daydream once you are deep in it. You must catch it as it starts.

We Help By: Sending subtle "checks" during vulnerable times to bring awareness back.

2. Anchor to Reality

Willpower isn't enough. You need to physically ground your nervous system.

We Help By: Providing 30-second somatic exercises (sight, touch) to instantly lower cortisol.

3. Rewire the Brain

Repetition is key. Every time you return, you weaken the addiction pathway.

We Help By: Tracking "Return Reps" to build real dopamine instead of fake fantasy.
